Pogány Induló - KETTŐ / KETTŐ (Official Music Video)

Pogány Induló - KETTŐ / KETTŐ is a visually striking music video set against the backdrop of Naples, Italy. The video explores the duality of this ancient city, contrasting its beautiful horizon with its gritty urban landscape. The aesthetic captures both the golden sunset and teal tones of the Mediterranean while incorporating stark white typography that splits the frame.
