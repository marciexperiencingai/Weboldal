ABOUT

Balázs is a director born in Pécs, embarked on his creative journey after completing his studies at the Metropolitan University of Budapest. His early career was marked by a passion for music videos, where he successfully crafted over 30 captivating projects.

Now, he has shifted his focus towards advertisement productions, leveraging his expertise in cinematography to create immersive, original fantasy worlds that seamlessly blend science fiction with realism. As a commercial director, Balázs has had the privilege of collaborating with esteemed brands such as Hell, Fizz!, Unicum, and TV2. His dedication to innovative storytelling continues to inspire and captivate audiences alike.

He has been honored with prestigious awards, including:

Best Music Video at Hungarian Music Video Awards (2022) - Slow Village's "Storytell"
Best Music Video at Hungarian Music Video Awards (2024) - Pogány Induló's "Úgyhiszem"
